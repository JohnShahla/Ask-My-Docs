# Orbit Analytics — Product Handbook

## Overview

Orbit Analytics is a product analytics platform that helps teams understand how
users move through their applications. It captures events, builds funnels, and
surfaces retention insights without requiring a data engineering team.

## Pricing

Orbit offers three plans:

- **Free** — $0/month. Up to 10,000 tracked events per month and 1 seat.
- **Pro** — $49 per user per month. Unlimited events, funnels, and dashboards.
- **Enterprise** — Custom pricing. Includes SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated
  customer success manager.

Annual billing receives a 20% discount on the Pro plan.

## Support

Support tiers follow the plan:

- **Free** — Community forum and documentation only.
- **Pro** — Email support with a 24-hour response-time target.
- **Enterprise** — Dedicated Slack channel and a named support engineer.

## Security & Compliance

Orbit is **SOC 2 Type II certified** and audited annually. The platform is also
GDPR compliant. All customer data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in
transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.

## Data Retention

When a customer deletes their account, all associated customer data is
**permanently deleted within 30 days**. Backups are purged on the same schedule.
Customers on the Enterprise plan may negotiate a custom retention window.
